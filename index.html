The 16th ISTE Annual Student Convention is a forum for exchanging scientific and technical innovative concepts and emphasizing the role of students in converting intelligence with excellence. The convention provides an ambient platform for both academicians and industrialists for a collaborative work and to discuss technical advancements and to promote innovations and invention for conducive lifestyle in the postmodern era. The convention comprises of several technical events to encourage their expertise in knowledge and creativity matching with global needs.
Theme
  The theme of the convention is “Promoting Innovative Practical Ability and Employability Skills in Engineering Education ”.
Subthemes
  – Electronics System Design and Communication for Next Generation
  - Power Electronic Circuits for Green Energy Technologies
  - Technological Revolutions of Social Media
  - Virtual Manufacturing
  - ICT for rural development
