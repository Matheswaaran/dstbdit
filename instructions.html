HOW TO REGISTER

Instructions to the Participants

1. No registration fee.

2. Registration Strictly Online.(Limited participants only - FCFS Basis).

3. Only on proper submission of tickets, TA shall be provided based on the Govt.Norms (II Class train Fare/State own buses - Non A/C)

4. Accomodation shall be provided for outside participants on request (Free Accomodation).

5. Registration forms (hard copy) should be properly attested by respective Head of the Institution. Click here to Download.

6. Participants are advised to follow formal dress code and bring the College ID proof.

7. No DA will be provided.

8. Faculty members, Ph.D scholars, PG & UG students are eligible to participate.
